Bespoke Financial Engineering

For financing challenges that don't fit into conventional boxes, our Structured Finance team provides creative and robust solutions. We specialize in transforming illiquid assets into tradable securities and designing products to manage specific risks.

  • Securitization: Pooling various types of contractual debt and issuing securities backed by these asset pools.
  • Asset-Backed Financing: Providing financing against a wide range of assets, including future revenue streams and intangible assets.
  • Credit Enhancement: Structuring solutions to improve the credit profile of an entity or transaction to achieve better financing terms.
