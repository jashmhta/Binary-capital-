From Aspiration to Achievement

Binary Capital began with a vision to create a new kind of financial advisory firm in Mumbai—one that prioritized long-term partnerships over transactional relationships. Our founders, veterans of global investment banking, saw an opportunity to bring world-class strategic thinking to the dynamic Indian market, but with a crucial difference: a truly bespoke, client-centric approach.

For over a decade, we have been transforming aspirations into achievements. We have guided companies through landmark M&A deals, secured critical growth capital, and structured innovative financial instruments that have powered expansion and created enduring value. Our history is not measured in years, but in the success stories we have helped write.
